Artemis

Goddess of Hunting, Wild Nature and Chastity

Sister of Apollo, Artemis was born to a human woman named Leto, her father being Zeus. Artemis was a patron of girls and young women, along with a protectress during childbirth. One of her most famous cult sites was one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient world, the Temple of Artemis at Ephesus.
